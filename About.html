Our Mission

To mould all-rounded holistic individuals-professionally sound, socially responsible and spiritually aware- who embody noble values and a right attitude, through Educare that caters to the emotional and psychological, intellectual, physical, and spiritual dimensions of the human personality.

Our Vision

To assist generations of students acquire self knowledge and self confidence, so as to cultivate self sacrifice and earn self realization; thereby molding them into leaders who will benefit society.
